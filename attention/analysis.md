# Attention Analysis

## Head 1: Layer 2, Head 8

This attention head appears to focus on determiners and the nouns that follow them. In multiple examples, words such as "the" and "a" attended strongly to the noun immediately after them.

Example sentences:
- The cat sat on the [MASK].
- I picked up a [MASK] from the table.

## Head 2: Layer 8, Head 4

This attention head appears to focus on pronouns and the verbs associated with them. Pronouns such as "he", "she", and "they" consistently attended to nearby verbs.

Example sentences:
- She quickly [MASK] home.
- They [MASK] the game yesterday.
